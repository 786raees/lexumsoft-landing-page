# LinkedIn Content Calendar

## Posting Schedule

Post every day. Optimal windows by day:

| Day | Best Time (EST) | Content Style |
|-----|-----------------|---------------|
| Monday | 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM | Pain-point / pattern interrupt (start the week with urgency) |
| Tuesday | 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM | System spotlight / how-it-works (early-week education) |
| Wednesday | 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM | Online presence / SEO / reviews (midweek optimization) |
| Thursday | 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM | Client proof / case study (social proof before weekend) |
| Friday | 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM | Math-driven ROI / revenue gap (owners tallying the week) |
| Saturday | 9-11 AM | Lighter / behind-the-scenes / industry trend (weekend scroll) |
| Sunday | 10 AM-12 PM | Weekly insight / founder perspective / seasonal prep (reflective tone) |

**Content style is a suggestion, not a rule.** Any angle can go on any day. The styles above reflect when each type tends to perform best for B2B contractor audiences on LinkedIn.
