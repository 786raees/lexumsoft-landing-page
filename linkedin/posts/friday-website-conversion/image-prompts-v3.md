### Key Rules for All Image Prompts

1. Every prompt must produce a complete, final image.
2. All text described with exact wording, font style, weight, color, size, and placement.
3. Brand bar at the bottom of every image.
4. Amber-gold accent lines wherever a separator or highlight is needed.
5. Use color names, not hex codes, in the prompt body.
6. Maximum 2-3 text overlay elements per image.

## Prompt 1 -- "The Bounce in Real Time"

### Platform and Model

**Primary:** Google Gemini 3 Pro (gemini-3-pro-image-preview)

Hybrid photo + UI overlay scene. A homeowner in a real domestic setting trying to contact a plumber through a terrible website, with floating UI elements showing the website's failures and the visitor bouncing. Same cinematic quality and UI overlay approach as the "After-Hours Save" image.

**Visual Impact:** High
**Priority:** 1 (Recommended)

---

### Prompt

```
IMPORTANT: This image combines a PHOTOREALISTIC scene with DIGITAL UI OVERLAY elements floating in the scene -- like a cinematic movie still merged with a software interface. The style blends real-world photography with tech dashboard elements layered on top, similar to a sci-fi movie HUD or a modern SaaS marketing hero image.

SCENE AND ENVIRONMENT: A cinematic, photorealistic scene inside a modern kitchen at approximately 6:30 AM. Square format, 1:1 aspect ratio. Early morning blue-gray light comes through a window. A woman in her late 30s sits at a kitchen island counter, holding her smartphone with both hands, staring at the screen with a frustrated, impatient expression. She is wearing a casual hoodie. On the counter beside her: a coffee mug with steam rising, and a crumpled paper towel. Behind her, slightly out of focus, a hallway is visible where a small puddle of water on the hardwood floor hints at the plumbing emergency that woke her up. The overall scene is dark and moody -- early morning, kitchen lights not fully on, lit primarily by the phone screen glow on her face and the blue dawn light from the window.

FLOATING UI OVERLAY ELEMENTS (rendered as semi-transparent digital panels floating in the scene, as if this is an augmented reality view):

ELEMENT 1 -- WEBSITE AUDIT PANEL (upper-right area of the image, floating at an angle):
A dark semi-transparent rounded rectangle panel (like a glassmorphism card) with a subtle amber-gold border glow. Inside the panel:
- Header: A small amber-gold dot followed by "Site Audit" in white text
- Below: Three rows, each with a small red "X" icon followed by white text:
  - "Phone number below fold"
  - "No mobile click-to-call"
  - "Contact form on separate page"
- At the bottom of the panel: "Conversion Rate: 2.1%" in amber-gold text

ELEMENT 2 -- BOUNCE INDICATOR (middle-right, below the audit panel):
A smaller floating panel showing:
- "Visitor leaving..." in muted gray text
- A progress bar that is nearly full, glowing red-orange
- "8 seconds on page" in small white text below

ELEMENT 3 -- COMPETITOR NOTIFICATION (lower-right, partially cut off by the edge):
A small notification-style floating card:
- "Competitor site: Call connected in 3 taps" in small white text
- A small green checkmark icon

A thin amber-gold curved line connects from the woman's phone screen outward to the floating UI panels, as if the data is being extracted and visualized from what she's seeing on her phone. This line has small glowing dots along it (like data flowing).

CAMERA: Medium-wide shot at approximately 35mm equivalent, f/2.0. The woman and her phone are in sharp focus in the left-center of the frame. The floating UI panels on the right are rendered crisply. The kitchen background and hallway with the water puddle are in soft bokeh.

LIGHTING: The primary light source is the phone screen casting a cool blue-white glow on the woman's face and hands. Secondary light is the blue-gray dawn light from the kitchen window. The floating UI panels emit a subtle amber-gold glow that casts faint warm light on nearby surfaces. The overall mood is dark, moody, and urgent -- a real emergency happening in early morning quiet.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark moody blues and grays for the kitchen scene. The amber-gold from the UI panels provides the only warm accent. The phone screen casts cool blue light. The contrast between the cold scene and the warm amber UI elements creates visual tension.

TEXT OVERLAY ELEMENTS RENDERED DIRECTLY ON THE IMAGE:

At the very top of the image, spanning the full width, with a subtle dark gradient overlay behind it for readability:

Line 1 (HEADLINE): "She needed a plumber. Your site lost her in 8 seconds." -- white, approximately 32 point, clean modern geometric sans-serif bold, centered

BRAND BAR (bottom 12% of the frame): Solid near-black charcoal bar spanning full width. A thin 2-pixel amber-gold accent line at the top edge. LexumSoft logo (attached as image file -- do NOT describe the logo) in bottom-left, approximately 40-50 pixels tall, white. "lexumsoft.com" in muted stone gray, bottom-right, 16 point.

MOOD: This is the moment a contractor loses a job without ever knowing it existed. The homeowner is real, the emergency is real, the frustration is real -- but the floating UI panels reveal what's happening underneath: the website is failing her in real time. Every contractor scrolling LinkedIn has a website just like this. The UI overlay makes the invisible visible -- it shows the data behind the bounce that contractors never see.
```

---

### Regeneration Notes

- **If UI panels are garbled:** Simplify to just ONE panel with "Conversion: 2.1%" and three red X icons. Remove the bounce indicator and competitor notification.
- **If the connecting line doesn't render:** Remove it entirely. The panels floating near the phone still read as connected to her experience.
- **If text inside panels is unreadable:** Make the panels larger and reduce text to just key numbers: "2.1%", "8 sec", and red X icons.

## Prompt 2 -- "The Optimization Dashboard"

### Platform and Model

**Primary:** Google Gemini 3 Pro (gemini-3-pro-image-preview)

Hybrid photo + UI overlay scene. A contractor in his truck reviewing website performance data, with floating UI elements showing the before/after conversion metrics and the specific fixes that drove the improvement. Same hybrid cinematic + tech overlay style.

**Visual Impact:** High
**Priority:** 2

---

### Prompt

```
IMPORTANT: This image combines a PHOTOREALISTIC scene with DIGITAL UI OVERLAY elements floating in the scene -- like a cinematic movie still merged with a software dashboard. The style blends real-world photography with tech interface elements layered on top.

SCENE AND ENVIRONMENT: A cinematic, photorealistic scene inside the cab of a contractor's work truck at golden hour (late afternoon). Square format, 1:1 aspect ratio. A male contractor in his mid-40s sits in the driver's seat, turned slightly toward a tablet propped on the steering wheel. He wears a faded work polo with rolled sleeves, and his hands show the grime of a day's work. He has a slight smile -- the expression of someone seeing good news in their numbers. Through the windshield, warm golden sunlight filters in from a suburban neighborhood. On the dashboard: a pair of work gloves, a coffee thermos. The truck interior is lived-in and real -- not clean or staged.

FLOATING UI OVERLAY ELEMENTS (rendered as semi-transparent digital panels floating in the scene):

ELEMENT 1 -- BEFORE/AFTER CONVERSION CARD (upper-right area, large and prominent):
A large dark semi-transparent glassmorphism panel with an amber-gold border glow. Inside:
- Two side-by-side metric blocks:
  - LEFT BLOCK: "BEFORE" label in muted gray at top. Large "2.1%" in muted gray below. "22 leads/mo" in small muted gray text. A small red downward arrow icon.
  - RIGHT BLOCK: "AFTER" label in amber-gold at top. Large "3.9%" in bright amber-gold below. "71 leads/mo" in small white text. A small green upward arrow icon.
- Between the two blocks: a white arrow pointing from left to right
- Below both blocks: a thin amber-gold horizontal line, then "First month results" in small white text

ELEMENT 2 -- FIXES CHECKLIST (middle-right, below the conversion card):
A smaller floating panel showing the optimizations made:
- Header: "Changes Made" in white text with a small amber-gold wrench icon
- Four rows with green checkmark icons:
  - "Click-to-call above fold"
  - "Contact form on every page"
  - "Trust badges added"
  - "Service area pages live"

ELEMENT 3 -- REVENUE BADGE (floating near the bottom-right):
A small rounded pill-shaped badge with amber-gold background:
- "+$14,200/mo" in near-black bold text inside the badge
- A subtle glow around the badge

A thin amber-gold curved line connects from the tablet screen to the floating panels, with small glowing data dots flowing along it.

CAMERA: Shot from the passenger seat perspective at approximately 35mm equivalent, f/2.0. The contractor and his tablet are in focus on the left side. The floating UI panels fill the right side of the frame. The windshield and suburban background are in warm bokeh. The angle is slightly below eye level, looking slightly up at the contractor, making him feel empowered.

LIGHTING: Warm golden hour sunlight streams through the windshield, casting long warm light across the truck interior and the contractor's face. The tablet screen glows with a dark-themed interface. The floating UI panels emit a subtle amber-gold glow. The warmth of the golden hour light combined with the amber-gold UI creates an optimistic, successful mood -- the opposite of the cold, frustrated "bounce" scene.

COLOR PALETTE: Warm golden tones from the sunset light. Dark truck interior. Amber-gold dominates the UI elements. The contractor's skin has warm golden tones. Everything feels warm, successful, and profitable. The amber-gold is everywhere -- in the sunlight, in the data, in the revenue badge.

TEXT OVERLAY ELEMENTS RENDERED DIRECTLY ON THE IMAGE:

At the very top of the image, with a subtle dark gradient overlay for readability:

Line 1 (HEADLINE): "Same site. Same traffic. 3x the leads." -- white, approximately 32 point, clean modern geometric sans-serif bold, centered

BRAND BAR (bottom 12%): Solid near-black charcoal bar spanning full width. Thin 2-pixel amber-gold accent line at top. LexumSoft logo (attached as image file -- do NOT describe the logo) in bottom-left, approximately 40-50 pixels tall, white. "lexumsoft.com" in muted stone gray, bottom-right, 16 point.

MOOD: This is the "after" moment -- the contractor who took action and is now seeing the results. The golden hour light makes everything feel warm and prosperous. The floating UI panels show concrete proof: the specific changes made and the specific results achieved. The revenue badge (+$14,200/mo) is the emotional climax. A contractor scrolling LinkedIn sees a version of himself -- sitting in his own truck, looking at his own numbers -- and thinks: "I want that."
```

---

### Regeneration Notes

- **If UI panels garble:** Simplify to ONE panel showing just "2.1% --> 3.9%" with "22 --> 71 leads" below it. Remove the checklist and revenue badge.
- **If the connecting data line fails:** Remove it. The panels floating near the tablet still read as connected.
- **If revenue badge doesn't render:** Replace with text: "+$14,200/mo" in amber-gold text floating near the bottom-right.
- **If the golden hour lighting doesn't render well:** Change to "warm overhead interior truck cab light" -- still warm, just artificial instead of sunset.
