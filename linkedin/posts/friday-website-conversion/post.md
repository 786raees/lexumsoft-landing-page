Same Google spend. Same traffic. Same crew. Wildly different revenue.

Because a web dev built it three years ago and nobody's touched it since. The phone number is in the footer. The contact form is on a separate page. There are no trust badges, no service area pages, no click-to-call button on mobile.

You're not a web designer. You run a plumbing or HVAC company. Nobody told you this stuff mattered.

It matters more than almost anything else in your marketing.

Click-to-call button above the fold. Contact form visible on every page without scrolling. Trust badges and licensing info front and center. Dedicated service area pages for each city he covers. Mobile layout rebuilt for one-thumb navigation.

First month: 71 form submissions. 3.9% conversion rate. $14,200 in new revenue from the exact same traffic.

Completely distinct stages of the funnel. This post is upstream; speed-to-lead is downstream.
  - This post does NOT overlap with Wednesday's GBP post -- GBP optimization drives more visitors to the site. This post is about converting those visitors once they arrive. Different system, different funnel stage.
