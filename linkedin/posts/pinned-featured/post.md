## The Post (~2,920 characters)

```
We audited 47 plumbing company websites last month.

Here are the 5 mistakes we see over and over — and what to fix first.

---

MISTAKE #1: Your phone number is buried.

On most plumbing sites, it's tucked in the footer or the top-right corner in tiny text. On mobile — where 70% of your visitors are — that number is invisible.

If someone's toilet is overflowing at 8pm, they're tapping the first number they can find. If that's your competitor's site, that job is gone.

Fix: Put a click-to-call button above the fold on every page. Make it impossible to miss.

---

MISTAKE #2: You're running ads to your homepage.

A homepage is not a landing page. It has a menu, your company history, links to every service you offer — 14 different places to go and no clear reason to call.

Across the 47 sites we audited, the ones running Google Ads to their homepage converted at 1.2%. Dedicated landing pages? 6.8%.

Fix: Build a single-purpose landing page for each service. One offer. One CTA. No navigation. Watch your cost-per-lead drop.

---

MISTAKE #3: Zero social proof above the fold.

You have reviews. Your customers love you. But your website doesn't show it until someone scrolls past your about section and your service grid. By then, most visitors have already left.

Fix: Put a star rating ("4.9★ — 127 Google Reviews") in your hero section, right next to your phone number. That single change has moved the needle for client after client.

---

MISTAKE #4: Your site loads in 5+ seconds on mobile.

Google's own data: 53% of mobile users leave a site that takes more than 3 seconds to load. A customer with an emergency doesn't wait. A slow site is a lost job.

Fix: Run your site through Google PageSpeed Insights right now. If you score below 70 on mobile, this is your first project. Speed isn't a bonus — it's table stakes.

---

MISTAKE #5: You don't have an offer. You have a list of services.

"We offer plumbing, drain cleaning, water heaters, emergency service..."

That's not an offer. That's a menu.

An offer gives the visitor a reason to choose you TODAY over every other plumber in their area.

Fix: Pick one clear offer for your homepage. "Emergency response in under 60 minutes" or "Upfront pricing — no hidden fees" or "Licensed, insured, background-checked technicians." One claim, stated boldly, backed by proof.

---

These are fixable. Every single one.

We've helped 200+ plumbing and HVAC companies work through these exact problems. Average result: 458% increase in qualified leads. 847% ROI on marketing spend.

The fixes aren't magic. They're just specific.
```
