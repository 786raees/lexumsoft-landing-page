# First Post — Image Prompts & First Comment

Post: "12 leads/month to 67 leads/month" Marcus case study.

---

## Image Specs

- **Recommended size:** 1200 x 1200 px (square — best engagement on LinkedIn)
- **Alt option:** 1200 x 627 px (landscape)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG

---

## Prompt Option 1 — Before/After Stat Card (Recommended)

Best performer for case study posts. The dramatic number contrast stops the scroll.

```
Square social media post graphic for a digital marketing agency case study, dark warm charcoal-black background #1A1714 with subtle geometric angular texture.

Large bold white sans-serif text at the top: "12 → 67"
Below that in smaller white text: "Leads Per Month"
Below that in amber-gold #C2710C text: "In Just 90 Days"

Bottom of the image has a thin amber-gold horizontal line separator, below it small muted gray text: "Plumbing Company Case Study"

Premium dark luxury aesthetic, clean minimal layout, matte finish, no photos no people no stock imagery no clip art, professional and bold.
```


## First Comment

Post this as the **first comment** immediately after publishing (within 30 seconds):

```
If your plumbing or HVAC company isn't getting the leads it should, we'll audit your website, Google presence, and ad setup for free — no pitch call required.

Book yours here: lexumsoft.com

Or just comment AUDIT and I'll send it to you directly.
```

> **Why the first comment?** LinkedIn's algorithm suppresses posts with external links in the body by ~60%. Putting the link in the first comment keeps your reach intact while still capturing clicks.
