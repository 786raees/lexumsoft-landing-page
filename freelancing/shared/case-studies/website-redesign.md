# ProFlow Plumbing & Heating -- 5x More Leads from Website Redesign

**Category:** Website Design
**Timeline:** 30 days

## Problem

ProFlow had a website built in 2008. It was not mobile-friendly, had no clear calls to action, and took over 8 seconds to load. Despite decent traffic, the site converted at just 1.2%. Thousands of dollars in potential revenue were walking out the door every month because visitors hit the back button within seconds.

## Solution

- Designed a modern, mobile-first website with click-to-call buttons above the fold on every page
- Added trust-building elements -- review widgets, certifications, licensing badges, service area coverage
- Optimized page speed to under 1.5 seconds load time
- Structured every page as a conversion funnel with clear CTAs and lead capture forms

## Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Conversion rate | 1.2% | 6.1% | +408% |
| Monthly leads | 8 | 41 | +413% |
| Page load time | 8s | 1.4s | -83% |
| Bounce rate | 72% | 32% | -56% |

## Testimonial

"Our old website was embarrassing. The new one looks incredible and actually gets us leads. We went from 8 leads a month to over 40 without spending a dime more on ads." -- David Chen, Founder

## Summary

ProFlow's outdated website was converting at 1.2% despite receiving steady traffic. We rebuilt it with a mobile-first, conversion-optimized design -- click-to-call placement, trust signals, fast loading, and clear CTAs on every page. Within 30 days, conversion jumped to 6.1% and monthly leads went from 8 to 41. Same traffic, 5x the results. No additional ad spend required.
