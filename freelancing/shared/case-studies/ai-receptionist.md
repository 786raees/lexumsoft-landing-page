# Elite Plumbing Services -- 100% Call Answer Rate with AI Receptionist

**Category:** AI Receptionist
**Timeline:** 14 days

## Problem

Elite Plumbing was missing 40% of incoming calls, mostly during evenings, weekends, and holidays. Each missed call represented a $500 to $2,000 job walking straight to a competitor. The owner knew he was bleeding revenue but could not justify the cost of a full-time after-hours receptionist or an answering service at $4,200 per month.

## Solution

- Deployed an AI receptionist to answer all after-hours calls with a natural, professional greeting
- Configured lead qualification workflows -- job type, urgency, location, contact info
- Set up automated callback scheduling for non-urgent requests
- Built an emergency dispatch system that routes urgent calls (burst pipes, no heat, gas leaks) to the on-call technician within minutes

## Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Call answer rate | 60% | 100% | +67% |
| After-hours leads (weekly) | 5 | 14 | +180% |
| Monthly revenue from AI | $0 | $12K | New revenue |
| Emergency response time | 2+ hours | 8 minutes | -93% |

## Testimonial

"The AI receptionist pays for itself ten times over every month. We went from missing half our calls to never missing one. It sounds so natural that customers don't even realize it's AI." -- Marcus Elite, Owner

## Summary

Elite Plumbing was losing 40% of calls after hours, with each missed call worth $500 to $2,000. We deployed an AI receptionist in 14 days that answers every call, qualifies leads, books appointments, and dispatches emergencies. Answer rate hit 100%, after-hours leads nearly tripled, and the system added $12,000 per month in previously lost revenue. Emergency response time dropped from over 2 hours to 8 minutes. The AI pays for itself ten times over.
