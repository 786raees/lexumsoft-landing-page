# Westside Plumbing -- Page 5 to #1 on Google

**Category:** SEO / Online Presence
**Timeline:** 6 months

## Problem

Westside had a basic website that had not been updated in years. They sat on Page 5 of Google for all their target keywords and generated zero organic leads. The entire business depended on expensive ads and dwindling referrals -- an unsustainable position that was getting worse every quarter.

## Solution

- Fully optimized their Google Business Profile -- photos, categories, service descriptions, business hours, Q&A
- Built 60+ directory citations across major platforms for local authority
- Created service area pages targeting each city in their coverage zone with keyword-rich, buyer-intent content
- Implemented a review generation system to build social proof and local ranking signals

## Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Google ranking (main keyword) | Page 5 | #1 | -- |
| Organic traffic (monthly) | 120 | 1,128 | +840% |
| Organic leads (monthly) | 0 | 45 | New channel |
| GBP views (monthly) | 800 | 5,200 | +550% |

## Testimonial

"We went from invisible to the #1 plumber on Google in our city. The organic leads alone replaced what we used to spend $4,000 a month on ads to get." -- James Westfield, Owner

## Summary

Westside Plumbing was buried on Page 5 of Google with zero organic leads, relying entirely on paid ads and referrals. We optimized their Google Business Profile, built 60+ citations, created location-specific service area pages, and launched a review generation system. Within 6 months they ranked #1 for their main keyword, organic traffic increased 840%, and they gained 45 organic leads per month -- a brand new channel that replaced $4,000 per month in ad spend.
