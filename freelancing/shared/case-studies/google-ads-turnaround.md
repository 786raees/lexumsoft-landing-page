# Johnson's Plumbing Co. -- 458% Lead Increase with Google Ads Rebuild

**Category:** Google Ads
**Timeline:** 60 days

## Problem

Johnson's was spending $2,000 per month on Google Ads managed by a generic agency. They were getting clicks but barely any phone calls. Cost per lead sat at $180, and most inquiries were for services they did not even offer. The ad account was a mess -- broad keywords, no negative keyword lists, and a single ad group targeting everything.

## Solution

- Rebuilt the entire Google Ads account with plumbing-specific keyword targeting and service-level ad groups
- Created comprehensive negative keyword lists to eliminate wasted spend on irrelevant searches
- Set up geo-fenced campaigns limited to their actual service area
- Built dedicated landing pages for each core service with click-to-call CTAs
- Installed call tracking and conversion attribution for every lead source

## Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Monthly leads | 12 | 67 | +458% |
| Cost per lead | $180 | $34 | -81% |
| Monthly revenue | $28K | $73K | +161% |
| Conversion rate | 2.1% | 11.8% | +462% |
