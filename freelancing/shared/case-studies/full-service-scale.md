# Premier Plumbing & Heating -- Solo Operator to 8-Truck Empire

**Category:** Full-Service
**Timeline:** 18 months

## Problem

Joe ran a one-man plumbing operation with a flip phone and a truck. No website, no online presence, no systems -- he survived entirely on word-of-mouth. He was turning away jobs because he could not handle the volume alone, but had no way to justify hiring without a predictable lead flow. Revenue sat at $15K per month with no path to growth.

## Solution

- Built a professional website from scratch with conversion-optimized design, service area pages, and lead capture
- Created and optimized a Google Business Profile with review generation from day one
- Launched geo-targeted Google Ads campaigns with plumbing-specific keyword strategy
- Deployed local SEO with 60+ citations and service area content
- Installed an AI receptionist to handle overflow and after-hours calls as volume grew
- Implemented a phased growth plan -- establish presence first, then scale ads and hiring in lockstep with lead volume
