**GBP Setup & Optimization**

## Upwork Fee Consideration

Upwork charges a 10% service fee on earnings. Factor this into all pricing decisions.

| Listed Price | Upwork Fee (10%) | Your Net |
|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| $700 | $70 | $630 |
| $1,600/mo | $160 | $1,440 |

When quoting custom projects, calculate your target net and add 10% to arrive at the listed price. Example -- if you need $900 net, list at $1,000.

## Upsell Path

The goal is to convert one-time project clients into long-term retainer clients.

**Stage 1: Entry Project ($700)**
Client hires for a single deliverable -- website build, GBP optimization, or ads setup. Deliver exceptional results. Over-communicate. Hit every milestone on time.

**Stage 2: Satisfied Client**
Results speak for themselves. Client sees more leads, better rankings, or lower CPL. Use the real-time dashboard to show concrete ROI numbers.

**Stage 3: Retainer Conversion ($1,600/mo)**
Propose ongoing management to maintain and compound results. Frame it around what they would lose by stopping -- rankings decay, ad accounts drift, competitors catch up.

**Stage 4: Referrals**
Happy retainer clients refer other contractors. Plumbing and HVAC owners talk to each other. One strong result generates 2-3 referrals on average.

**Full path:** $700 project --> satisfied client --> $1,600/mo retainer --> referrals

## Pricing Notes

- Never discount the project price. The $700 entry point is already competitive for the deliverables included. Discounting signals low value and attracts price-sensitive clients who churn.
- For larger scopes (multi-location, 10+ page sites, aggressive ad budgets), quote custom pricing based on the work required. Use $700 as the baseline for a standard single-location scope.
- Bundle pricing -- if a client wants all three services (website + GBP + ads), offer them as a $1,900 package (saving $200) to incentivize the full build. Your net after Upwork fees is $1,710.
- The retainer price ($1,600/mo) aligns with the agency's Growth tier ($1,997/mo) adjusted for freelancing context and Upwork fee structure.
