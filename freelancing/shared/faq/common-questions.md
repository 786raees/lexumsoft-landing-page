# Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. Why only plumbing and HVAC?

Because specialization beats generalization. We exclusively serve plumbing and HVAC companies -- no other industries. Every keyword list, ad template, website layout, and conversion strategy we use is built on performance data from 200+ contractors. Generic agencies spread themselves thin across dozens of industries and deliver average results. We go deep in one so you get strategies that are proven, not experimental.

## 2. What if I already have a website?

We can work with what you have. We will audit your current site and recommend the best path forward -- sometimes an optimization pass is all you need. One client went from 1.2% to 3.9% conversion with targeted improvements, no rebuild required. If a full redesign makes more sense, we will tell you why and show you the projected ROI before you commit.

## 3. How fast will I see results?

It depends on the service. Google Ads campaigns typically start generating calls within the first week of going live. Website redesigns show measurable conversion improvements within the first 30 days. SEO and GBP optimization build over 3-6 months, but we focus on quick wins first to deliver immediate ROI while the long-term strategy compounds.

## 4. Do you work outside the US?

We work with plumbing and HVAC companies across the entire United States. All strategies are tailored to your specific service area, whether you cover one city or fifteen. We do not currently serve businesses outside the US, as our data, benchmarks, and platform expertise are US-focused.

## 5. What is your revision policy?

For website projects, you get two rounds of revisions included in the project scope. We present the initial design, gather your feedback, and refine. Most clients are happy after the first revision because we follow proven contractor website frameworks -- we are not guessing at what works. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds can be handled at an hourly rate.

## 6. Can I start with just one service?

Yes. Many clients start with a single project -- a website build, GBP optimization, or Google Ads setup -- and add services once they see results. There is no requirement to buy a full package. That said, the five systems compound when they work together. Clients who use all five see significantly higher ROI than those using one or two in isolation.

## 7. How do you prove ROI?

Every client gets a real-time dashboard showing calls, form submissions, cost per lead, booked jobs, and ROI. We use call tracking, form tracking, and CRM integration to attribute every lead back to its source. No black box. No vanity metrics like impressions or clicks. You see the actual revenue generated by every dollar spent.

## 8. What if it does not work?

We offer a 90-day ROI guarantee. If you do not see a positive return on your investment within 90 days, we continue working for free until we deliver. We track every lead, call, and booked job for complete transparency -- so "working" is defined by your revenue numbers, not our opinion.

## 9. How are you different from other freelancers?

Three things. First, we only work with plumbing and HVAC companies -- every strategy is built on industry-specific data, not generic marketing theory. Second, we deliver five integrated systems (website, GBP/SEO, ads, reviews, AI receptionist) that compound results, while most freelancers sell one thing. Third, we back it with a 90-day ROI guarantee and no contracts. If we do not deliver, you pay nothing more.

## 10. Do I need to sign a contract?

No long-term contracts. For project-based work (website build, GBP setup, ad campaign setup), we use Upwork's milestone system -- you approve each phase before payment releases. For ongoing management, it is month-to-month. Cancel anytime. No hidden fees, no penalties. We earn your business every month.

## 11. What is the AI receptionist?

It is an AI-powered phone system that answers calls when your team is unavailable -- after hours, weekends, holidays, or during peak overflow. It uses advanced natural language processing to greet callers professionally, qualify leads by job type and urgency, book appointments into your calendar, and dispatch emergencies to your on-call technician. Customers cannot tell it is AI. It replaces expensive answering services at a fraction of the cost.

## 12. How do you handle communication?

For project-based work, we communicate through Upwork messaging with regular updates at each milestone. For ongoing management, you get bi-weekly or weekly strategy calls (depending on your plan), plus access to your real-time dashboard. We respond to messages within 24 hours on business days. You will never feel left in the dark about what we are doing or how it is performing.

## 13. What do I need to provide?

For a website build -- your logo, brand colors (or we guide you), service list, service area, team photos, and any content you want to keep. For GBP -- business details, service list, photos, and Google account access. For ads -- service list, service area, budget, and any existing ad account access. For AI receptionist -- company details, services, on-call contact info, and calendar access. We provide a simple onboarding checklist so nothing gets missed.

## 14. How much does it cost?

Project-based work starts at $700 per service (website build, GBP optimization, or Google Ads setup). Ongoing management runs $1,600 per month and includes ad optimization, SEO updates, GBP management, reporting, and AI receptionist management. We structure payments through Upwork milestones -- typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Exact pricing depends on scope, and we provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

## 15. Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After a project is delivered, you can continue on a monthly retainer for ongoing management -- ad optimization, SEO updates, GBP posting, review management, reporting, and AI receptionist tuning. Most clients who start with a one-time project move to ongoing management because the compounding results justify the investment. But there is no obligation -- if you just want the initial build, that works too.
