# Upwork Proposals Log

## Status Values

- **Sent** -- Proposal submitted, awaiting client action
- **Viewed** -- Client opened the proposal
- **Interviewed** -- Client initiated a conversation or interview
- **Hired** -- Contract started
- **Rejected** -- Client declined or hired someone else
- **Withdrawn** -- We withdrew the proposal
