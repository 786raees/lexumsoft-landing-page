## Notes

- Check alerts daily. First 1-2 hours after a job posts is the window where proposals get the most visibility.
- Prioritize jobs where the client has $1K+ total spent and payment verified -- these are serious buyers.
- Jobs with fewer than 5 proposals are highest priority. Submit within 30 minutes if possible.
- Also browse the "Best Matches" feed daily -- Upwork's algorithm surfaces jobs that match your profile skills even if they do not use your exact search terms.
- Avoid jobs with budgets under $300 or clients with no spending history unless the job description signals a serious buyer.
