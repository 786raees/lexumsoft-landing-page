## Description

Full-service digital marketing management for your plumbing or HVAC company. Everything running, optimized, and tracked -- so you can focus on running jobs instead of managing marketing vendors.

This retainer is for contractors who already have the foundation in place (website, ads, GBP) and need ongoing management to keep results growing. Or for clients who started with one of our setup packages and want us to run everything month to month.

Here is what happens every month. Your Google Ads campaigns get optimized -- bid adjustments, new negative keywords, ad copy A/B tests, budget reallocation based on which services are performing. Your SEO gets maintained and expanded -- new content, updated service area pages, fresh GBP posts, citation monitoring. Your Google Business Profile stays active with weekly posts, photo uploads, and Q&A management. Reviews get monitored and responded to. Your AI receptionist scripts get refined based on call data.

Every month, you get a report showing exactly what happened: calls received, form submissions, cost per lead by channel, booked jobs, and total ROI. No vanity metrics. No fluff. Just the numbers that matter to your business.

**Q: What if I only need some of these services?**
A: We can customize the retainer scope. If you only need ad management and reporting, or just SEO and GBP, we can adjust the deliverables and price accordingly. Message me to discuss what makes sense for your situation.
