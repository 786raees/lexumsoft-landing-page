## Description

A complete Google Business Profile optimization and local presence buildout for your plumbing or HVAC company. This is the single highest-ROI marketing investment most contractors can make -- one client saw a 73% call increase from 3 weeks of GBP optimization alone, with zero ad spend.

Most contractors have a half-finished Google Business Profile. One or two categories selected out of dozens available. Old hours. A handful of stock photos. No posts, no Q&A, no service descriptions. That means Google does not have enough information to rank you in the Map Pack -- the top 3 local results that get 50%+ of all clicks on local service searches.

We fix that. Every field, every category, every signal that Google uses to decide who shows up in the Map Pack gets optimized based on what we have seen work across 200+ plumbing and HVAC accounts.

Then we build your citation foundation. 60+ directory listings (Yelp, Angi, BBB, HomeAdvisor, industry-specific directories) with consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) data. Inconsistent citations confuse Google and hurt your rankings. We clean up existing ones and build new ones.

Next, we set up a review generation system. Automated text messages go out after every completed job asking for a Google review. We provide response templates for both positive and negative reviews. Companies with 200+ reviews at 4.8+ ratings dominate local search. Most contractors have 12-20 reviews.

**Westside Plumbing results:** Went from Page 5 to #1 on Google. Organic traffic from 120 to 1,128/month. Organic leads from 0 to 45/month. GBP views from 800 to 5,200/month.

**Greenleaf HVAC results:** Google reviews from 12 to 215. Rating from 3.8 to 4.9. Local pack ranking from #5 to #1. Inbound quote requests from 10 to 28/month.

This package gives you the local SEO foundation that makes every other marketing channel -- ads, website, referrals -- work harder. When someone Googles "plumber near me" and your profile shows up first with 100+ five-star reviews, you win that call.
