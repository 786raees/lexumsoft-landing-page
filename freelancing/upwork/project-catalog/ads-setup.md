## Description

A complete Google Ads campaign build for your plumbing or HVAC company -- from keyword research to conversion tracking. Built on data from 200+ contractor ad accounts, not generic PPC templates.

Most contractors running Google Ads are wasting 40% or more of their budget. The typical setup from a generic agency looks like this: one ad group with dozens of unrelated keywords, no negative keyword lists, broad match everything, one generic landing page, and zero conversion tracking. You are paying for clicks from tire-kickers, DIYers, people outside your service area, and searches for services you do not offer.

We build it differently. Every service gets its own ad group with its own keywords, ad copy, and landing page. Drain cleaning has different search intent than water heater replacement -- they need different ads and different landing pages. We apply plumbing/HVAC-specific negative keyword lists refined across 200+ accounts to eliminate wasted spend on day one. Geo-fencing ensures you only pay for clicks from people in your actual service area. And every call and form submission is tracked so you know exactly which keywords and ads are generating booked jobs, not just clicks.

**RedPipe Plumbing results:** Same $3,000/month budget. ROAS improved from 1.2x to 5.3x. The budget did not change -- the strategy did.

**Desert Cool HVAC results:** In the most competitive HVAC market in the country (Phoenix), we helped a small company capture 23% market share in paid search while actually spending 20% less on ads than the previous summer.

You get campaigns that are ready to generate calls within the first week of launch. No 3-month "learning period." No vague promises about "brand awareness." Calls and booked jobs from week one.
